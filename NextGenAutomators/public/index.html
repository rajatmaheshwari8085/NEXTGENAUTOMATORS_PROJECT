About Project

Welcome to our project Smart Multi-Utility IoT Automation & Monitoring System, an innovative solution designed to make everyday environments smarter, safer, and more efficient using Internet of Things (IoT) technology.

This system integrates multiple sensors and automation mechanisms to monitor and control different real-world conditions in real time. Our goal is to reduce manual effort, improve resource utilization, and provide a user-friendly interface for seamless monitoring and control.

Project Components

  • Soil Moisture Sensor – Detects soil conditions and automates irrigation systems
  • Ultrasonic Sensor (HC-SR04) – Identifies animal movement and enhances field security
  • DHT Sensor – Monitors temperature and humidity, enabling automatic control of devices like cooling fans
  • Web-based Dashboard – Displays live data and allows users to control appliances remotely

Key Features

  • Real-time data monitoring
  • Automation and smart decision-making
  • Energy efficiency and resource optimization
  • User-friendly interface for easy access and control

This project has been developed by a team of engineering students passionate about IoT and embedded systems. It reflects our commitment to solving real-world problems using technology and innovation.

We aim to further enhance this system by integrating advanced analytics, cloud storage, and AI-based decision-making in the future.

Our Mission

To develop smart, efficient, and scalable IoT solutions that simplify daily life and improve productivity in agriculture and home automation.

Our Vision

To contribute towards a smarter and connected world by leveraging the power of IoT and automation technologies.
